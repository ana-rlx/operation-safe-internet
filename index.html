Activity Overview

This activity introduces students to basic cybersecurity concepts and safe online behavior. Students learn to identify threats like phishing and weak passwords through practical tasks and reflection.

What Is Cybersecurity?

Protecting your digital life is a superpower! Check out the tips below.

Strong Passwords

Combine letters, numbers, and symbols. Example: Sky!Blue77#

Phishing

Watch out for strange emails with "Urgent" warnings or bad spelling!

Internet Safety Challenge

Answer all 5 questions to earn your Certificate!

1. A popup says your computer has a virus and you must click to "Clean Now." What do you do?
2. You want to download a cool new game. What is the safest way?
3. You are choosing a password for your email. Which is best?
4. Someone you don't know follows you on social media and asks for a selfie. What do you do?
5. Once you post a photo or comment online, who can see it?
