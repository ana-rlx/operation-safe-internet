Cybersecurity Essentials

üõ°Ô∏è What is Cybersecurity?

Protecting your computers, phones, and private information from dangers online.

Why it matters: Hackers try to steal data, scams trick users, and some sites are dangerous.

üîë Create Strong Passwords

A strong password is your digital shield. Remember these tips:

  • Use 8‚Äì12 characters.
  • Mix LETTERS, numbers, and symbols!
  • Never use your name or "123456".
SECURE EXAMPLE: Tiger!9Moon#

üé£ Spot the Phish!

Phishing = Fake messages trying to trick you. Red Flags:

  • Strange sender email address
  • Spelling or grammar mistakes
  • "Urgent! Action Required!" messages

üåê Protect Your Space

Keep accounts private, ask before downloading, and remember: Once it's online, it's there forever.
